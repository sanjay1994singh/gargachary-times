Privacy Policy for Gargachary Times

Last Updated: June 2026

Gargachary Times ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Gargachary Times mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

The Gargachary Times mobile application does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing information
  • Payment information

Users can access news content, e-paper content, and live news broadcasts without creating an account.

News Content and Live Streaming

The application provides access to:

  • News articles
  • News categories
  • E-paper content
  • Live news broadcasts

During live broadcasts, viewers may see advertisements, promotional content, sponsored messages, or commercial announcements that are part of the broadcast stream.

Device and Technical Information

Like most online services, our servers may automatically receive certain technical information such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser or device information
  • Date and time of requests

This information is used for service operation, security, troubleshooting, and performance monitoring.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services for content delivery, video streaming, hosting, and infrastructure support. These services may process technical information necessary to provide the service.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security and integrity of our services. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The application is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the application. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
